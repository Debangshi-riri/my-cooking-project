Crunchy, salty, and a little bit spicy.

The Dressing

  • 1.5 tbsp Soy Sauce, 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar, 1 tsp Sugar
  • Garlic, Chili crisp (optional)

Instructions

Slice cucumbers thinly. (Optional: salt and drain them first). Mix dressing ingredients, toss with cucumbers and spring onions. Chill for 15 mins before serving.
