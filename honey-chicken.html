Lacquered, spicy, and sweet. Thighs > Breasts.

The Sauce

  • 2 tbsp Gochujang, 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1.5 tbsp Soy Sauce, 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • Garlic, Ginger, Sesame Oil

The Process

Coat chicken in cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Fry until crispy. Simmer the sauce until glossy, then toss the chicken in it until fully coated. Serve over rice with a soft-boiled egg.
