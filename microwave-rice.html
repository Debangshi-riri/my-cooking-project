My signature 1:2 soy-vinegar dark sauce recipe.

1. The Base

Microwave 1/2 onion, 1.5 green onions, and mustard oil for 1:30 mins.

2. The "Dark Sauce"

  • 2 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp Vinegar
  • Mom's Masalas (Garam Masala, Coriander, Cumin)
  • Salt & a pinch of sugar

3. The Cook

Add 1/2 cup rice, veggies (carrots/cabbage), sauce, and 1 cup water. Microwave with lid (vented) for 10 mins.

4. The Finish

Stir, add green chilies. Microwave 2 mins (no lid). Add 1/4 cheese slice, cover, and let sit in microwave for 3 mins.
