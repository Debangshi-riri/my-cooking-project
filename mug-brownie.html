The 5-minute sweet tooth cure.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Flour, 2 tbsp Cocoa, 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 1-2 tbsp Oil/Butter, 3 tbsp Milk
  • Pinch of salt, Vanilla (optional)

Instructions

Mix everything in a mug until smooth. Microwave for 1 minute (check at 45s). It should be soft in the middle!
